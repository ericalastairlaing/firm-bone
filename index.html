Description

A team of adventurers lands in a pirate port, "Plate-Rock Port", only to find the mothers of the community vanishing without a trace. Suspicion points toward Oldberth Crag, a perilous, dagger-shaped island that looms out of the sea.

World Lore

"The free people of the Inner Orion Seas are finding refuge in darker places and dangers lurk atop and beneath the waves...

The Orinator, a soulless corporate magnate dead-set on extracting rare minerals and resources, and Ancient Terrors that do not yet lose purchase in this world."

Campaign Summary

In the vast, unending waters of the Inner Orion Seas, survival is a daily struggle. The last bastions of the free people are scattered across remote islands and hidden coves, constantly fleeing the shadow of The Orinator, whose corporate fleets bleed the world of its rarest resources. In his wake, leaving the systematic eradication of any culture that dares to stand in his way. But the greed of mortals is not the only darkness here. Beneath the waves and in the salt-fog, ancient terrors stir, their grip on the world far from broken.

"Your story begins in Plate-Rock Port, a rough-and-tumble pirate haven that offers a semblance of safety from the Orinator patrols..."

But a new, insidious fear has taken root. One by one, the mothers of the community are vanishing without a trace, spirited away in the dead of night. Panic gives way to grim suspicion, and all eyes turn to the horizon, toward the jagged silhouette that tears at the sky: Oldberth Crag.

As a team of adventurers seeking fortune or purpose, you arrive to find a community gripped by terror. Can you unravel the mystery of the missing women? Will you brave the treacherous shores of Oldberth Crag and confront the evil that dwells there, or will the secrets of the crag, and the sea itself, swallow you whole?

Who are you in this world?

Choose a path or forge your own. Let these whispers guide your history.

Born & Raised

The Inner Orion is your home, and Plate-Rock is your sanctuary. One of the missing women is your kin, your mentor, or your mother. You don't need fortune—you need answers.

Defector

You were once a cog in the Trading Company machine. You’ve seen the "resource extraction" for what it really is: slaughter. You’re in hiding now, but your skills could save this port—if you're willing to step into the light.

Seeker

You’ve spent your life studying the ancient texts of the Inner Orion. You know that the vanishings aren't just a crime; they are a ritual. You came to Plate-Rock because the stars told you the Crag is waking up.

Survivor

Your ship went down near the jagged rocks of Oldberth Crag. You were the only soul to make it to the shore. You remember a song in the fog, and a shadow beneath the waves. You came to Plate-Rock to forget, but the dreams won't let you.
